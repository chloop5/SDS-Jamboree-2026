About the Event

The SDS Jamboree 2026 is a weekend gathering bringing together all the members of the Association, scouts and leaders for an unforgettable experience of adventure, teamwork, and learning. From Friday May 15th 2026 to Sunday May 17th 2026, participants will take part in a variety of activities designed to challenge skills, build friendships, and celebrate the spirit of scouting. With participants aged 7+, supported by dedicated adult leaders and youth team leaders, the event will include outdoor challenges, skill workshops, creative projects, Scout Concert and campfire. Our goal is to create a safe, inclusive, and well-organized environment where every participant can enjoy the full program without long waits or missed opportunities. Whether you’re guiding a patrol, building camp structures, cooking for the crew, or capturing the moments, your role is vital to making this jamboree a success!
